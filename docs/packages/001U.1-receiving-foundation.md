# 001U.1 — Receiving Foundation

## Outcome

001U.1 introduces BTSP's physical receiving ledger and separates warehouse evidence from vendor logistics events.

## Capabilities

- Store-scoped, sequential receipt numbers (`RCV-YYYY-NNNNNN`).
- Receipt lines linked to immutable purchase-order lines and optionally to ASN lines.
- Exact received, accepted, and rejected quantity accounting.
- Required rejection reasons and lot-number capture.
- Store-scoped external receipt IDs with canonical SHA-256 replay protection.
- Active-store, eligible-PO, PO-line, store-line, ASN, and ASN-line validation.
- Cumulative accepted quantity projection to `partially_received` and `received` PO states.
- Append-only receipt snapshots without duplicating receipt contents.
- System-admin receiving permissions and store-authority enforcement for future receiving roles.
- Receiving workspace at `/receiving`.

Physical overages are deliberately recorded. Classification, tolerance policy, and exception routing belong to 001U.2.

## API

- `POST /api/v1/receipts`
- `GET /api/v1/receipts`
- `GET /api/v1/receipts/{receipt_id}`

Writes require `receiving.manage`; reads require `receiving.read`. Non-system users are constrained to their assigned home store.
