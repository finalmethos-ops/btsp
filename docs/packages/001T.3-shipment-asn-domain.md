# 001T.3 — Shipment Updates and ASN Domain

## Delivered

- Vendor shipment headers and immutable shipment-update projections.
- Planned, in-transit, delayed, delivered, and cancelled shipment states.
- Carrier, tracking, location, and delivery-date metadata.
- Advance Ship Notices linked to acknowledged Purchase Orders and optional shipments.
- ASN lines linked directly to PO lines with product identity validation.
- Cumulative ASN quantity enforcement that prevents advising more than ordered.
- Vendor, PO, shipment, event-type, state-transition, and idempotency controls.
- PO projection to shipment lifecycle states without asserting warehouse receipt.
- Immutable shipment and ASN snapshots.

## API

- `POST /api/v1/vendor-integrations/events/{id}/process-shipment`
- `POST /api/v1/vendor-integrations/events/{id}/process-asn`
- `GET /api/v1/vendor-integrations/shipments`
- `GET /api/v1/vendor-integrations/asns`

## Boundary

Vendor shipment delivery means the vendor reports transport delivery. It does not create a receipt,
inventory movement, invoice match, or reconciliation result. Those authoritative warehouse and
financial operations begin in Epic 001U.
