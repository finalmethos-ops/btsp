# 001V.1 — Analytics Foundation and Operational Dashboard

## Outcome

001V.1 introduces a read-only KPI contract and dashboard spanning the current BTSP operational lifecycle.

## Metrics

- Purchase-order count and status distribution.
- Ordered spend grouped by currency.
- Receipt count and accepted/rejected quantities.
- Open receiving variances, open backorders, and outstanding backorder quantity.
- Invoice count, status distribution, invoiced amount by currency, and line-match exception count.
- Reconciliation case/status counts and open exception count.

## Access

- API: `GET /api/v1/analytics/operational-dashboard`
- UI: `/analytics`
- Permission: `analytics.read`
- Role: `ANALYTICS_VIEWER`

The endpoint is read-only and exposes aggregate operational data. Store, region, vendor, date-period, and organizational filters follow in later 001V packages.
