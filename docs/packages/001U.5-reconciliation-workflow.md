# 001U.5 — Three-Way Reconciliation and Exception Workflow

## Outcome

001U.5 turns stable PO, receipt, and invoice evidence into controlled reconciliation decisions and an auditable approval-for-payment boundary.

## Case creation

One reconciliation case may exist per invoice. Creation is idempotent and generates separate exceptions for:

- invoiced quantity versus cumulative accepted receipt quantity;
- invoiced versus ordered unit price;
- invoiced versus ordered freight;
- invoiced versus ordered tax.

Clean cases enter `ready_for_approval`; cases with discrepancies enter `exception_review`.

## Exception workflow

Every exception must receive a note and one disposition:

- `accept_variance` — authorized acceptance of the difference;
- `vendor_credit` — credit is requested/expected;
- `corrected_invoice` — replacement invoice evidence is expected.

When all exceptions are dispositioned, the case becomes ready for approval. This transition does not approve payment.

## Final decisions

- Approval is permitted only from `ready_for_approval` and projects the invoice to `approved_for_payment`.
- Rejection is permitted before a final decision, including while exceptions remain open, and projects the invoice to `rejected`.
- Final decisions cannot be changed.
- Case creation, exception clearance, approval, and rejection retain actor, note, prior status, and resulting status.

## API and UI

- `POST /api/v1/reconciliations`
- `GET /api/v1/reconciliations`
- `POST /api/v1/reconciliations/exceptions/{id}/resolution`
- `POST /api/v1/reconciliations/{id}/decision`
- Finance workspace: `/invoices`

Visibility requires `reconciliation.read`; dispositions and decisions require `reconciliation.manage`.
