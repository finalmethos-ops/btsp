# 001U.4 — Invoice Ingestion and Line Matching

## Outcome

001U.4 records immutable vendor invoice claims and produces deterministic line-match evidence against purchase-order pricing and cumulative accepted receipt quantities.

## Controls

- Vendor invoice numbers are unique per vendor and protected by canonical SHA-256 replay checks.
- Header subtotal must equal invoice lines; total must equal subtotal, freight, and tax.
- Every line extension must equal quantity multiplied by unit price.
- Invoice vendor and currency must match the purchase order.
- Invoice lines must reference unique lines on that purchase order with the same product code.
- Created, prepared, and cancelled purchase orders cannot be invoiced.

## Match evidence

Each invoice line snapshots:

- ordered, accepted, and invoiced quantities;
- invoiced minus accepted quantity difference;
- ordered and invoiced unit prices;
- invoiced minus ordered price difference;
- `matched` or `exception` status and match timestamp.

These values are not recomputed when later receipts arrive. 001U.5 reconciliation will use this stable evidence to route and resolve exceptions.

## API and UI

- `POST /api/v1/invoices`
- `GET /api/v1/invoices`
- `GET /api/v1/invoices/{invoice_id}`
- Invoice workspace: `/invoices`

Invoice reads require `invoices.read`; ingestion requires `invoices.manage`.
